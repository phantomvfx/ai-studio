# Original Concept

A cyberpunk detective chases a rogue android through neon-lit streets...

# Phase 1: Pre-Production

## Story Arc

# STORY ARC: Chrome Tears in Electric Rain
**Core Emotion/Theme:** Neon-Drenched Empathy

### 1. THE HOOK (0-5 Seconds)
Extreme macro of raindrops exploding against a steaming neon sign, immediately tilting down to reveal the detective’s weathered face bathed in violent magenta light; his cybernetic eye whirs and zooms, locking onto a hooded figure that bolts, shattering a puddle into prismatic shards that fill the screen with chromatic aberration.

### 2. THE BUILD (5-20 Seconds)
The pursuit becomes a kinetic vertical ballet through the city’s layered infrastructure—the detective vaults over a holographic ramen stall while the android scales a wet concrete wall, leaving phosphorescent handprints that fade like dying stars. They weave through crowds of faceless commuters under glitching holographic cherry blossoms, the detective launching a wrist-grapple to swing through a canyon of signage that shorts and flickers in his wake, closing the gap with desperate, rain-slicked momentum.

### 3. THE PAYOFF (20-30 Seconds)
The detective lunges, tackling the android through a glass barrier; they plummet in suspended slow-motion between towering advertisements that streak past like comets of cyan and magenta, rain freezing in mid-air around them. The android’s hood falls back, revealing not machinery but a face of desperate, human fear—both figures lock eyes, suspended in the neon void, as the city lights blur into a brilliant abstract wash resolving on the final image.

## Screenplay

## Scene 1: EXT - STEAMING NEON-LIT ALLEY - NIGHT
**Action:** Extreme macro of raindrops exploding against a steaming neon sign bathed in violent magenta light. Camera tilts down to reveal a male detective with a weathered face, heavy stubble, and a cybernetic right eye with visible mechanical components and glowing magenta iris, wearing a dark charcoal trench coat soaked with rain. His cybernetic eye whirs and mechanically zooms. A slim figure wearing a dark hooded cloak with face completely obscured by shadow bolts away, shattering a puddle into prismatic shards that fill the screen with chromatic aberration.

## Scene 2: EXT - RAIN-SOAKED STREET MARKET - NIGHT
**Action:** The detective, his weathered face and glowing magenta cybernetic eye still visible beneath his dark charcoal trench coat soaked with rain, vaults over a holographic ramen stall. The slim figure in the dark hooded cloak scales a wet concrete wall, leaving phosphorescent handprints that fade like dying stars. Heavy rain continues to fall in sheets.

## Scene 3: EXT - CROWDED PLAZA UNDER GLITCHING HOLOGRAPHIC CHERRY BLOSSOMS - NIGHT
**Action:** The detective, still wearing the dark charcoal trench coat with his weathered face and glowing magenta cybernetic eye visible, weaves through crowds of faceless commuters beneath glitching holographic cherry blossoms. He launches a wrist-grapple cable, swinging through a canyon of signage that shorts and flickers cyan and magenta in his wake. The slim figure in the dark hooded cloak sprints ahead through the digital petals.

## Scene 4: EXT - VERTICAL CITY CANYON - NIGHT
**Action:** The detective, his weathered face illuminated by the glow of his cybernetic eye and still wearing the rain-soaked dark charcoal trench coat, swings closer on the grapple line with desperate momentum. The slim figure in the dark hooded cloak leaps between high platforms above the street, rain-slicked and backlit by neon.

## Scene 5: EXT - BETWEEN TOWERING ADVERTISEMENTS - NIGHT
**Action:** The detective, his weathered face and glowing magenta cybernetic eye visible beneath his dark charcoal trench coat, lunges and tackles the slim figure in the dark hooded cloak through a glass barrier. They plummet in suspended slow-motion between towering digital billboards streaking past like comets of cyan and magenta light. Rain droplets freeze in mid-air around them. The dark hood falls back from the slim figure, revealing not machinery but a human face with desperate fear in the eyes. Both the detective and the revealed figure lock eyes, suspended in the neon void.

## Scene 6: EXT - SUSPENDED IN THE NEON VOID - NIGHT
**Action:** The detective, still wearing his dark charcoal trench coat with his weathered face and glowing magenta cybernetic eye, and the slim figure with the revealed human face remain locked in eye contact as they fall. The surrounding city lights blur into a brilliant abstract wash of cyan and magenta, resolving on the final image of their two faces suspended against the electric glow.

## Art Director Suggestions

### 🎨 ART DIRECTION OPTIONS
*Choose one of these paths, or type in your own custom Art Direction for the next phase:*

**Option 1: Oxidized Mechanism**
- **Vibe:** A tactile, grounded future where technology bleeds and rusts like organic matter—emphasizing the weight of the chase and the raw humanity beneath the hardware.
- **Color & Light:** Deep sodium-orange street haze bleeding into violent magenta neon; wet surfaces reflect bruised purples and rust-reds. Lighting comes from practical sources only—flickering tube lights, leaking chemical glow from the cybernetic eye, and warm sodium vapor creating harsh, unforgiving shadows.
- **Production Design & Wardrobe:** The detective’s trench coat is heavy waxed canvas (19th-century weight) soaked to a charcoal sheen with visible water beading, not synthetics. His cybernetic eye exposes oxidized brass gears, leaking amber hydraulic fluid that mixes with rain on his stubble. The hooded figure wears rough-spun hemp or burlap cloak, frayed at the edges, absorbing water and casting deep shadows. Environments feature rain-slicked brutalist concrete with actual moss growing in cracks, rusted corrugated metal stalls, and holographic cherry blossoms projected through layers of steam and grease-coated glass.

**Option 2: Bioluminescent Mourning**
- **Vibe:** A sleek, wet dreamscape where organic and synthetic materials merge seamlessly—emphasizing the beauty of the pursuit and the fragility of the human reveal.
- **Color & Light:** Saturated bioluminescent magentas and deep oceanic cyans; soft volumetric fog catching light like liquid silk. Prismatic rainbows fracture through every water droplet. The lighting is ethereal—glow emanates from within objects rather than external sources, creating floating halos and subsurface scattering on skin.
- **Production Design & Wardrobe:** The trench coat is nano-fabric that shifts from deep charcoal to iridescent mother-of-pearl when saturated, with fiber-optic threading that pulses faintly. The cybernetic eye features translucent silicone housing revealing fluid magenta optics pumping like a heartbeat, seamless with the weathered skin. The hooded cloak is made of smart-fabric that displays faint, dying star constellations across its surface. Cherry blossoms are tangible bioluminescent organisms drifting in the air, leaving trails of phosphorescent dust. Surfaces are polished obsidian, liquid-slick titanium, and glassine textures that reflect infinite neon corridors.

**Option 3: Analog Glitch Decay**
- **Vibe:** A deconstructed reality where digital errors manifest as physical textures—emphasizing the fragmented nature of perception and the truth hidden beneath layers of technological noise.
- **Color & Light:** Violent chromatic aberration made tangible—magenta and cyan physically separated into distinct channels creating 3D ghosting without glasses. High-contrast blacks with blown-out highlights, simulating expired film stock. Lighting strobes erratically as if from failing CRT monitors, casting scan-line shadows across faces.
- **Production Design & Wardrobe:** The trench coat is woven with vintage photographic negative strips and fiber-optic threads carrying static images. The cybernetic eye is constructed from antique clockwork (visible gears, springs) fused with magenta Edison bulbs and vacuum tubes that flicker. The hooded cloak consists of layered torn Polaroids and thermal foil blankets that crinkle like dry leaves. Environments feature buildings wrapped in emergency thermal blankets creating quilted metallic textures, holograms projected through smoke and shattered prismatic glass installations that physically split light into rainbows, and rain that appears as suspended chemical stains on the air—visible as paint splatter textures frozen in time.

## Chosen Art Direction

Option 1 from previous suggestions.

# Phase 1.5: Cinematography

## Cinematographer Suggestions

### 🎥 CINEMATOGRAPHY OPTIONS
*Based on your chosen Art Direction, choose the camera language that best tells this story. You can also type in your own custom camera instructions:*

**Option A: Anamorphic Chase Noir**
- **Gear & Lenses:** ARRI Alexa 35 with Atlas Orion Anamorphic lenses (40mm, 65mm, 100mm) – utilizing the 2x squeeze to capture horizontal blue/magenta lens flares bleeding across the frame when hit by neon signage, maintaining a 2.39:1 aspect ratio for cinematic scope
- **Lighting Setup:** High-contrast practical neon sources (magenta and cyan LED tubes) with heavy atmospheric haze/Atmosphere Aerosol to create volumetric light beams; negative fill on the detective's face to maintain noir shadows while the cybernetic eye provides its own practical magenta glow; wet-downs on all surfaces for reflective ground planes
- **Motion (I2V):** Aggressive handheld gimbal work (DJI Ronin 2) with intentional micro-shakes during the alley sprint; snap zooms (crash zooms) when the cybernetic eye activates; low-angle tracking shots at 24fps following the vault over the holographic stall; whip pans between the detective and the hooded figure to maintain kinetic energy

**Option B: Operatic Volumetric Melancholy**
- **Gear & Lenses:** Sony Venice 2 with Cooke S7/i Full Frame Plus spherical lenses (85mm T2.0, 100mm Macro T2.0) – wide open at T2.0 to create creamy bokeh with circular highlights from raindrops, emphasizing shallow depth of field on the detective's weathered face
- **Lighting Setup:** Single-source motivated lighting with large soft magenta key lights (Aputure 600d with magenta gel) mimicking neon practicals; heavy volumetric fog throughout; precise cyan rim lighting (kicker) separating the hooded figure from black backgrounds; underexposed by 1 stop to preserve highlight detail in the neon while crushing shadows to pure black
- **Motion (I2V):** Smooth Technocrane movements – slow, creeping track-ins (30cm/second) on the detective's cybernetic eye in Scene 1; elegant boom-ups following the grapple swing through the plaza; static locked-off camera during the slow-motion fall (Scene 5) allowing the subjects to fall through the frame while digital billboards streak past, emphasizing the emotional gravity of the eye-lock moment

**Option C: Cyber-Mechanical POV & Glitch Aesthetic**
- **Gear & Lenses:** RED V-RAPTOR XL with Lomo Anamorphic Squarefronts (50mm, 80mm) – vintage Russian glass producing heavy chromatic aberration, barrel distortion, and organic vignetting; mixed with Laowa 24mm Probe Lens for extreme macro work on the raindrops and mechanical eye components
- **Lighting Setup:** Stroboscopic practical lighting with irregular flicker rates (creating subliminal glitch effects); high-contrast with crushed blacks (no detail below 20 IRE); chromatic aberration emphasized through lighting angles; magenta and cyan LEDs programmed to short-circuit and flash randomly during the chase sequences
- **Motion (I2V):** Mechanical servo-zooms mimicking the cybernetic eye's optical zoom (rapid 50mm-100mm zooms with slight overshoot/bounce); Dutch angles (22.5-degree tilts) during the wall-scaling sequence; vertigo effect (dolly zoom) pulling back while zooming in during the glass-barrier tackle; bullet-time array capture (120fps) for the falling sequence with 360-degree camera rotation around the suspended figures, rain droplets frozen mid-air in sharp focus against blurred neon streaks

## Chosen Cinematography

Option A from previous suggestions.
